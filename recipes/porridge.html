CREAMY PORRIDGE

Healthy porridge with greek yogurt

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 spoons of porridge
  • 3-4 spoons of greek yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • pomegranate
  • optionally: strawberries

Steps:

  1. mix porridge with greek yogurt
  2. leave for about 5-10 mins
  3. check if oatmeal is soft
  4. add bananas and mix them in
  5. add pomegranate to your liking
  6. optionally add a few sliced strawberries
  7. mix everything
  8. add 1-2 teaspoons of muesli on top
  9. eat and enjoy
