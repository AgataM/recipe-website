BANANA PANCAKES

Delicious pancakes made with two ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. put the banana and eggs into a blender
  2. blend until there's only liquid
  3. put a spoon of olive oil or any type of oil of your liking on a pan
  4. take the mixture and pour it onto the pan forming small thin pancakes
  5. when the bottom side get browned flip the pancake
  6. optional toppings: greek yogurt and fruit
  7. eat and enjoy
