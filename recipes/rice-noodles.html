Rice noodles recipes

Plain chilli noodles

Spicy noodles

Ingredients:

  • rice noodles
  • chilli flakes
  • salt
  • soy sauce
  • seasame oil
  • sriracha sauce

Steps:

  1. cook the rice noodles
  2. add chilli flakes and salt to your liking
  3. add sauce
  4. eat and enjoy

Sauce recipe:

Mix two teaspoons of soy sauce, one teaspoon of seasame sauce and 3 drops of sriracha sauce.
